LEVEL 3 · 45–67s · PRECISION FILTERING
🎨
Two Reference Colours Per SessionPress ONLY when 7 appears in one of the 2 reference colours. A 7 in any other colour = false alarm.
Reference colours: CYAN & PINK
✓ 7 in CYAN → press → correct hit
✓ 7 in PINK → press → correct hit
✗ 7 in YELLOW → do NOT press → false alarm
Strategy: Keep one eye on the colour indicator in the HUD. Don't react to 7 alone — check its colour first.
LEVEL 4 · 67–90s · NEGATIVE INTERFERENCE
➖
Positive vs Negative NumbersNegative numbers appear in red. Press ONLY positive +7.
✓ 7 (positive) → press → correct hit
✗ −7 (negative, red) → do NOT press
✗ −3 (negative, red) → do NOT press
Strategy: Read the full number before pressing. The negative sign (−) is the trap.
